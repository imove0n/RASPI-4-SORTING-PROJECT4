HOW YOLO TRAINING WORKS:
=========================

Simple explanation:
-------------------
1. You show YOLO 100 photos of tomatoes
2. You tell it where tomatoes are (boxes)
3. You tell it what type (ripe/unripe/rotten)
4. YOLO learns what tomatoes look like
5. YOLO can now find tomatoes in new photos!

Technical (what happens):
-------------------------
1. Neural network learns tomato features
2. Learns shape, texture, color patterns
3. Learns to ignore background
4. Optimizes over 100 epochs
5. Creates detection model
