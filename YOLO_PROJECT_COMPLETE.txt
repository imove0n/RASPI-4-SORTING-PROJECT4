WHAT HAPPENS ON RASPBERRY PI:
==============================

When you run yolo_tomato_detector.py:

1. Loads your trained model.pt
2. Opens camera feed
3. Runs YOLO detection in real-time
4. Draws boxes ONLY around tomatoes
5. Labels each: "ripe" or "unripe"
6. Shows confidence scores
7. Counts detections
8. Ignores all non-tomato objects! ✅
