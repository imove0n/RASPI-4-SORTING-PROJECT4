TROUBLESHOOTING:
================

Issue: Camera not detected
Fix: Check ribbon cable connection
     Blue side faces USB ports

Issue: "No tomato detected" in classifier
Fix: Adjust lighting, get closer
     Make sure tomato fills more of frame

Issue: Wrong classification
Fix: Adjust color thresholds in script
     Or wait for YOLO (learns from data)

Issue: YOLO install fails
Fix: Network timeout - retry later
     Or use simple classifier for now
