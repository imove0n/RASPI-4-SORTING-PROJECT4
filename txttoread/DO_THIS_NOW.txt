STEP 4: Label Images (20 minutes)
----------------------------------
For EACH image (repeat 40 times):

1. You see an image
2. Click "Bounding Box" tool (rectangle icon on left)
3. Click and drag around the tomato:
   ┌──────────┐
   │   🍅     │  ← Draw tight box
   └──────────┘
4. Type the label:
   - "ripe" if tomato is red/orange
   - "unripe" if tomato is green
5. Press Enter
6. Click "Save" (or Ctrl+S)
7. Click "Next" (or press 'n')
8. Repeat!

SPEED TIP:
- Draw box → Type label → Press Enter → Press 'n'
- 30 seconds per image = 20 minutes total!


STEP 5: Generate & Export (2 minutes)
--------------------------------------
After labeling all 40 images:

1. Click "Generate" (top menu)
2. Augmentation settings:
   ☑ Check "Flip: Horizontal"
   ☑ Check "Rotation: ±15°"
   ☑ Check "Brightness: ±15%"
   (This creates more training data!)
3. Click "Generate"
4. Wait 1-2 minutes
5. Click "Export"
6. Format: Choose "YOLOv8"
7. Click "Download ZIP"
8. Save to your computer
9. Done!
